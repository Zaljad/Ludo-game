Game Interface
1.	The game board is shone
2.	The player’s turn is shone above the board
3.	An exit icon to exit the game is shone
4.	Each player has two pointers
Game Start & Turns
5.	The user clicks on the dice to start the game
6.	To git the pointer on the boarder to start play the player needs to roll the dice and git 6 in the dice
7.	If the dice didn’t show 6 the player can’t get his pointer on the game board and the play turn will be switched to the other player
8.	Every single pointer needs a 6 to get onto the board
9.	If a player gets 6 in the dice, he will have a chance to re roll the dice and play with total sum of his dice rolls
10.	If a player gets 6 three times in the sequentially then he will not be able to play the turn and the turn will be switched to the other player
11.	The number that shows in the dice will represent the number of grids that the pointer will move inside the game board
12.	If the dice show 6 the player can re roll the dice again to play otherwise the turn will be changed to the other player
13.	The players can’t step inside the other player’s separate path (that grids will be on the color of the player pointer)
14.	If the grids that remind less than the number that shone in the dice the player will not be able to play this turn and the turn will be switched to the other player
Player Interaction Rules
15.	If a player stands in a grid and the other player comes to the same grid the first player pointer will be back out of the game board
16.	There is a safe grid that even if both players stand on it no pointer will out of the game board
Board Paths & Zones
17.	There is a separate path for each player after completing the course around the board
18.	Players have different start zones and end zones
Winning Condition
19.	The player that enters all his pointers to his pointers finish zone will be the winner
